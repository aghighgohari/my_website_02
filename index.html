Concept

This website works like a small digital archive, bringing selected works together in a clear and accessible way. The design focuses on structure, consistency, and ease of use, allowing users to experience the content as part of a growing collection. By keeping things simple and organized, the site shows how digital spaces can function as well-curated repositories of information.

Unroll History is an interactive digital archive tracing the evolution of Persian rugs over five centuries — from the Safavid court to contemporary workshops. The experience unfolds as a series of chapters, each shaped by its own context, visual language, and way of making. From royal precision to tribal memory, from global trade to modern revival, each era reflects a shift in how rugs were designed, produced, and understood. Rather than presenting history as a fixed timeline, the site invites visitors to move through it — exploring how culture, craftsmanship, and meaning are woven into every piece, knot by knot.
